Hello recruit, welcome to your ghost hunting training.
I will be your guide robot during your training.
At the end of your training you will be part of the UN-GHOST squad.
Let's start with the basics.
You move with the A W S D keys or the arrow keys.
The weapon you carry in your right hand fires a laser beam that damage the ghosts. To use it you must press the right mouse button.
The weapon you carry in your left hand, aspires them to you. To use it you must press the left mouse button.
Don't be afraid, they can't hurt you.
To finish the trainig kill them, and interact (pressing the E key) with the recipient that are in the zone.
Good luck newbie!
